Health and social services centre (CSSS), with local branches (CLSCs), promotiong the health and welfare of the local population.
Bilingual volunteers (3rd language an asset) are needed to serve as rehabilitation assistants helping out department staff. Volunteers will greet patients, encourage them in their exercise and rehabilitation program and help with equipment in order to promote healthy recovery. Volunteers must meet with the coordinator of volunteer services, provide two references and undergo a police check. | Place of work: Richardson Hospital, CSSS Cavendish and Catherine Booth Hospital
